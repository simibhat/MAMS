About

Military of a country plays a very vital role in securing safety of its citizen. An airport primarily for military use is called an airbase or air station. A military airport is an aerodrome used by a military force for the operation of military aircraft. Airports often have facilities to store and maintain aircraft, and a control tower.
Air safety is an important concern in the operation of an airport. There have been many aviation accidents and incidents due to pilot error, mechanical failure, weather, sabotage and other forms of human error. Effective database management system is needed to combat these problems to certain extent. For efficient and smooth functioning of airport also it is important.
